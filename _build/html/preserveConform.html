Preserve/Conform SelectionsÂ¶

Preserve and Conform Selections are options for the user to enhance the capabilites of Selectors. Preserve Selections causes the model that is currently rendered to remain while a selection is being made. Conform Selections causes the selector to only select from models that are currently rendered.
